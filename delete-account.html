Delete Your Account – Kernels App

To delete your Kernels account:

  1. Open the Kernels app
  2. Tap on your Profile
  3. Go to Settings
  4. Tap Delete My Account
  5. Type DELETE to confirm

Once confirmed, your account and all associated data (articles, bookmarks, endorsements, etc.) will be permanently deleted. This action is irreversible.

We do not retain your data after deletion. If you have issues deleting your account, please contact: your@email.com
